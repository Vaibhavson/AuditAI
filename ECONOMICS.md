# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. A typical deal: a startup spending $1,000/mo on Claude/ChatGPT switches to Credex credits at 25% discount = $250/mo savings for them, ~$100–150/mo margin for Credex.

Assuming a 12-month contract: **LTV per customer = ~$1,200–1,800**

Conservative estimate used below: **$1,200 LTV per converted customer**

## CAC at Each Channel

| Channel | Effort | Est. CAC |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Hacker News Show HN | 1 post, ~2 hrs | ~$0 (time only) |
| Twitter/X organic | 3–5 posts/week | ~$0 (time only) |
| Reddit (r/startups, r/ExperiencedDevs) | 2 posts | ~$0 (time only) |
| LinkedIn cold DM | 20 DMs, 2 hrs | ~$0 (time only) |
| Paid Twitter/X ads (if scaled) | $500 budget | ~$50–80/lead |

At zero paid budget: **CAC ≈ $0 cash, ~$20–40 in founder time per lead**

## Conversion Funnel

```
Landing page visit       → 100%
Audit completed          → 40%   (400 audits per 1,000 visitors)
Email captured           → 25%   (100 emails per 1,000 visitors)
Consultation booked      → 10%   (10 bookings per 1,000 visitors)
Credit purchase closed   → 40%   (4 customers per 1,000 visitors)
```

**Break-even:** At $1,200 LTV and ~$0 CAC, first customer = profitable. At paid CAC of $50/lead → need >4% lead-to-close to break even. Current funnel estimate (10% booking × 40% close = 4%) is right at break-even — meaning any organic channel is pure profit.

## $1M ARR in 18 Months — What Has to Be True

**$1M ARR = ~833 active customers at $100/mo average margin**

Month 1–3: 50 customers (organic HN + Reddit launch)
Month 4–6: 150 customers (word of mouth + 1 vendor partnership)
Month 7–12: 400 customers (paid acquisition kicks in, CAC <$200)
Month 13–18: 833 customers (sales team closes enterprise deals)

**Key assumptions:**
- Average contract: $1,200/year ($100/mo margin)
- Churn: <5%/month (sticky if savings are real)
- Vendor partnership: 1 co-marketing deal with Cursor or Anthropic drives 200+ warm leads
- The audit tool drives 2,000+ audits/month by month 6 — 10% become consultation leads

**The math works if:** Credex closes 1 in 10 consultation bookings at $1,200 LTV and the tool generates 700+ consultations in 18 months. At 2,000 audits/month × 5% consultation rate = 100 consultations/month × 10% close = 10 new customers/month. 10 × $100 margin × 12 months = $12,000 MRR added per month. Month 18 MRR = ~$84,000 = ~$1M ARR. Achievable.
