## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no trial. You get the full audit instantly. We capture your email after showing you the results — never before.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every number is pulled from official vendor pricing pages and verified weekly. Sources are cited in our pricing data file. If something changes, we update it.

**Q: What do you do with my email?**
A: We send you a copy of your audit report. If your savings opportunity is significant, a Credex consultant may reach out to explain how discounted AI credits work. You can unsubscribe anytime.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — from companies that overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real and substantial. This audit tool is free because it helps us find people who could genuinely benefit.

**Q: Can I share my audit with my team?**
A: Yes — every audit gets a unique public URL. Your email and company name are stripped from the public version. Only the tools and savings numbers are shown.
